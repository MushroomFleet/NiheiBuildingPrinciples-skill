## Usage Notes

### Adapting the Template

- Not every section is required for every project
- Remove sections that don't apply (e.g., no visual component = de-emphasize geometry/lighting)
- Expand sections where the project has particular depth or need
- The Enhanced Plan (Part V) is the primary deliverable; Parts I-IV are supporting analysis

### Applicable Project Types

This template applies to any creative project, not just manga:
- Game design documents, level designs, environment concepts
- Film/animation scripts, storyboards, production design
- Novel/fiction world-building documents
- Architectural concept proposals and speculative designs
- Visual art direction guides and mood boards
- Tabletop RPG setting documents
- VR/immersive experience design documents

### Tone Guidelines

- Professional but not dry
- Confident in recommendations but respectful of original vision
- Never condescending about original plan's gaps
- Frame enhancements as opportunities, not corrections
- Reference Nihei works for inspiration, not imitation
- The goal is to amplify the creator's intent through Nihei's spatial philosophy, not to make everything look like Blame!

### Length Guidance

- Executive Summary: 3-5 sentences
- Assessment sections: Concise tables + brief analytical notes
- Enhancement sections: As detailed as needed for implementation
- Enhanced Plan: Complete and standalone (may be longest section)
- Appendices: Reference material, not critical reading
